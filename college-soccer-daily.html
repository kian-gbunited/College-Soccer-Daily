Spring Showcases

The major evaluation events where college coaches watch prospects. Spring runs April through June — the most important recruiting window of the year.

Key Spring 2026 Events
ECNL Boys — Indianapolis Showcase
April 17–20, 2026 · Grand Park, Westfield IN
Hundreds of D1 coaches
One of the premier spring evaluation events for U14–U19 boys. Grand Park is within driving distance of a significant number of college programs, which drives high coach turnout. ECNL Boys events consistently draw hundreds of scouts each season. Ongoing this weekend.
Girls Academy Spring Showcase
April 9–13, 2026 · Bryan Park, Greensboro NC
300+ college coaches
The GA Spring Showcase draws over 300 college coaches and scouts from across the country, plus US Soccer Women's Youth National Team staff. U15–U19 teams play non-conference opponents across five days. One of the most important spring windows for women's recruiting. Completed April 13.
ECNL Girls — Phoenix National Event
Spring 2026 · Phoenix, AZ
Hundreds of D1 coaches
ECNL National Events averaged hundreds of scouts each in the 2024–25 season — more than almost every other youth platform. The Phoenix event is one of the highest-attended ECNL girls events of the spring evaluation window. Completed.
MLS Next Flex
April 23–28, 2026 · Toyota Soccer Complex, Frisco TX
400+ college coaches
MLS Next Flex historically draws over 400 college scouts with representation from over 225 different schools. U15–U19 age groups compete across four days. Group winners qualify automatically for the MLS Next Cup. Critical spring event for players in the MLS Next ecosystem.
MLS Next Cup
May 23–31, 2026 · Regional Athletics Complex, Salt Lake City UT
Hundreds of scouts
The season-ending MLS Next championship with finals at America First Field, home of Real Salt Lake. 32 teams per age group qualify through league performance and Flex results. Major talent identification opportunity for college and professional scouts.
ECNL Boys National Playoffs
June 25 – July 2, 2026 · Surf Sports Park, San Diego CA
Highest D1 coach attendance of the year
The ECNL Boys National Playoffs are the single highest-concentration D1 coach evaluation event of the summer for boys. U13–U19 age groups. San Diego location draws coaches from programs across the West and nationally. The most important event on the boys recruiting calendar.
ECNL Boys National Finals
July 17–19, 2026 · Striker Park, Richmond VA
Elite evaluation window
The championship conclusion of the ECNL Boys postseason. Top teams from each age group compete for the national title. College coaches attend in large numbers — players here are competing for spots on coaches' final recruiting boards ahead of fall.
MLS Next Fest
December 2026 · Mesa, AZ (Annual)
500+ coaches · 1,400+ teams · 30,000 players
The largest youth soccer scouting and talent identification event in the United States. The 2025 edition featured over 500 professional, national team, and college coaches from approximately 250 collegiate programs, 1,474 teams, and over 30,000 players across 2,400 matches. The premier winter recruiting event in North America.
