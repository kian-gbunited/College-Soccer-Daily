Spring Showcases

The major evaluation events where college coaches watch prospects. Spring runs April through June — the most important recruiting window of the year.

Key Spring 2026 Events
ECNL Boys — Indianapolis Showcase
April 17–20, 2026
Grand Park Sports Campus, Westfield, Indiana. One of the premier spring evaluation events for U14–U19 boys. Draws college coaches from all three NCAA divisions. Ongoing this weekend.
ECNL Girls — Phoenix Showcase
April 10–13, 2026
Phoenix, Arizona. Completed. One of the highest D1 coach attendance events of the spring for women's recruiting.
Girls Academy Spring Playoffs
May 2026 · Location TBD
One of the top women's recruiting events of the spring. D1 coaches attend in large numbers to evaluate 2027 and 2028 prospects.
ECNL Boys National Championship
June 2026 · Location TBD
The highest-concentration D1 coach evaluation event for boys in the summer. Historically draws more D1 staff than any other single event on the calendar.
MLS Next Playoffs
June 5–15, 2026 · Nationwide
Strong D1 coach attendance. Players in MLS academy programs should confirm their playoff schedule with their club.
ECNL Girls National Championship
June 2026 · Location TBD
Major summer showcase for women's prospects. D1 coaches from programs across the country attend to evaluate top ECNL talent.
